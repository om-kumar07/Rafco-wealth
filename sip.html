Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)


SIP means Systematic investment plan and it is one of the most popular investment technique when you are planning to stay long in the market and your goals are defined.Its one of the investment plan that insured you guranteed returns.Invester needs to invest periodically for the certain period of time.SIP in one of the kind of mutual fund.Under SIP, an investor can invest in mutual funds in a disciplined manner, with a fixed amount invested regularly, irrespective of the market conditions. This helps to average out the cost of investment over time and helps the investor avoid the risk of timing the market.

     There are so much benefits of SIP , you should considering investing as early as possible.The present time is always right you just needs to get accuinted that return is guranteed.SIP gives you so much reliability about market fluctuation , you don't need to worry about your money just invest periodically.There are several reasons why the present time may be a good time to invest in SIP some of these are Potential for higher returns,Rupee cost averaging,Lower minimum investment requirements etc.


There are so much myths have been spread about SIP in the society , we want to clear all the general doubts about SIP.

Myths about SIP


SIP is for limited group


  One of the biggest myths about SIP is , it is limited for salaried class or small investers , but its not totally correct any can start SIP with minimum of Rs 100 and there is no cap for maximum .


Start SIP when market is high


  Should SIP started when market is high and stopped when market is low but its not the conclusive explanation of SIP , SIP doesn't work in that way its a time given investment.Market doesn't affect much more SIP.


SIP is magic


  Mutual fund or SIP doesn't work in that way , better return affected by many factors such as funds you are chossing and what time you're willing to spend.The point should be always noted that SIP is also a subject to market risk.


Start SIP at perfect time


   Its not precisely correct because when you look at the trend from past years how much returns SIP has provided , you would be amazed to say there is not any perfect time for it , present time is always better time to start.You should focus on invest for long term in diciplined manner.


SIP doesn't provide Lump Sum


   Many of people have percpective in mind that invester can't make lump sum investment when SIP is there but its not correct.


SIP dates are important


  As we all know SIP is long term investment so dates doesn't precisely matter much more , its just for the convenience of invester.


  Despite of all these myths evenso SIP is one of the most popular and reliable investment plan for long term .There are some common "Mantras" invester should consider in order to start the SIP.One should always consider these techniques if they don't have much more experience about SIP , but we argue to you that if you have any doubt please reach at us.


Mantra to get started with SIP


Start SIP

 Set your goals and invest proper amount periodically for certain period of time.SIP offers the benefit of compounding returns.As names suggest its a systematic or strategic investment technique.


Investing right amount

 Investing the right amount is important to achieve your financial goals through SIP.There are so much factors affecting in order to choose right amount these are financial goals,budget,investment hozon.If you are unsure about how much to invest in SIP, consult us we can help you determine the right amount based on your financial goals, budget, and investment horizon.


Invest in Regular basis

 Regular investing is important in SIP because it helps to create a disciplined approach to investing, which can lead to better outcomes over the long term.Set your goal start investing according to these.SIP helps to create a disciplined approach to investing, averages out market fluctuations, increases the power of compounding, and reduces the need to time the market.


Start early

 Starting early is one of the most important factors for a successful SIP investment. starting early is important for a successful SIP investment because it allows you to take advantage of the power of compounding returns, more time in the market, lower risk, and the ability to invest smaller amounts over a longer period.
